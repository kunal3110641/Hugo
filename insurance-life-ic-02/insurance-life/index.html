LIFE INSURANCE

Life Insurance can be defined as a contract between an insurance policy holder and an insurance company, where the insurer promises to pay a sum of money in exchange for a premium, upon the death of an insured person or after a set period.

[Policy Term:]{.underline}

The policy term is the total duration of your life insurance coverage.

[Premium Paying Term:]{.underline}

Premium paying term is the number of years for which the premium have to be paid.

NOTE: PPT (Premium paying term) <= PT (policy term)

[Sum Assured:]{.underline}

Sum assured is a fixed amount that is paid to the nominee of the plan in the unfortunate

event of the policy holder’s demise.

[Maturity Age]{.underline}:

Maturity age is defined as the minimum and maximum age of the user.

Example: Min age 23

= 18(age) + 5year(Min PT)

= 23 years (Mix maturity age)
