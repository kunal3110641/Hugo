What is Motor Insurance

● A safety cover for your vehicle

● Mandatory - Motor Vehicle Act 1988

● Regulator - IRDAI (Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India)

Break In

1. Suppose your policy is ending by 1st Sept 2021

2. You are supposed to renew it before it expire

3. But you do not renew

4. Now 2nd Sept 2021 onwards It will be called as “BreakIn”

So

1. Case can be breakin -

a. If you don’t have previous year policy

b. You have previous year policy but it has been expired

and you did not renew (as mentioned in above case)

Components of Premium

Premium = OD Premium + TP Premium + GST

● OD (Own Damage)

● TP (Third Party)

● GST

Own Damage Premium

Insurance against your own vehicle damage

Third Party Premium

Insurance against third party damage caused by your vehicle
