16. Additional Cover

1. Zero Depreciation

a. What is depreciation

b. Normally Claim = IDV - depreciation

c. If you have opted for Zero Depreciation

d. Claim = IDV - zero

All Additional Covers are part of OD Premium

2. Engine Protector

a. Normally in your insurance policy your vehicle’s engine is

not covered

b. You have opt for it explicitly

3. Consumables

a. Consumables = those materials of your car that serve a

specific purpose for a limited period of time

b. Ex. Engine oil, Gearbox oil, Nut Bolt etc.

c. Once used, consumed completely

d. Have to be refilled or replaced frequently because of high

tear and wear

e. Except Fuel

4. Key Replacement

a. Cover the cost of getting new set of keys

b. If keys are : Lost, Stolen or Damaged

5. Tyre Secure

a. Cover the expenses of replacement of tyre

17. 24*7 Road Side Assistance (RSA)

18. Return to Invoice (RTI)

a. Invoice Value = 40 lakh

b. IDV = 35 lakh

c. It covers the gap between Invoice value and I DV, in case of

total loss

19. Externally fitted CNG

a. It adds both OD and TP Component in the Premium

b. How much OD component ?

i. 4 % of the CNG Value

c. How much TP component ?

i. Fix: 60 Rs

d. Ex. CNG Value = 1000

e. OD component = 1000 ka 4 % = 40 Rs

f. TP component = fix = Rs. 60
